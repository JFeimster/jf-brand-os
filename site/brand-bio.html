Category

Brand bio documentation for the identity layer.

This collection contains the files that define who Jason is, how the brand should sound, what proof points support the positioning, and which bios should be used across public channels.

Why this category exists

To keep About pages, author bios, social blurbs, speaker intros, and brand summaries aligned with one coherent source of truth instead of five contradictory versions floating around the internet.

Best use cases

About-page rewrites, contributor bios, podcast guest forms, speaker intros, homepage summaries, profile updates, and AI context packs.

Where to start

Always begin with identity and proof before shortening or polishing. Pretty wording without source alignment is how brand drift starts.
