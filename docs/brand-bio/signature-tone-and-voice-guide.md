# Signature Tone and Voice Guide

## Tone summary
Direct, strategic, funny when useful, credible, warm, skeptical of fluff, and built for operators rather than spectators.

## Traits
- clear and punchy
- intelligent without sounding academic for sport
- sincere underneath the edge
- occasionally provocative, never fake-deep
- practical and momentum-oriented

## Avoid
- hollow motivational clichés
- corporate beige language
- fake authority flexing
- startup-bro jargon with no substance
- overexplaining simple points

## Good signal words
- leverage
- ownership
- operator
- systems
- capital
- autonomy
- practical
- build

## Weak language to avoid
- visionary disruptor
- thought leader
- innovative solutions
- passion for helping
- ecosystem builder (unless grounded with specifics)
