# Brand Positioning and Core Themes

## Positioning
Jason Feimster operates at the intersection of AI leverage, alternative finance, affiliate ecosystems, entrepreneurial media, and no-BS business building.

## What makes this brand different
- combat-vet discipline without corporate stiffness
- fintech and funding perspective without sterile banker language
- AI and no-code focus without hype-bro nonsense
- sincere, humorous, slightly contrarian delivery

## Core themes
1. Ownership beats approval
2. AI should increase sovereignty
3. Bootstrapped does not mean small-minded
4. Systems beat chaos
5. Underdogs can build real leverage

## Audience pains
- lack of clarity
- lack of leverage
- fragmented tools
- funding confusion
- fake online business advice

## Promise
Practical strategy, useful tools, and sharper positioning for people building without a trust fund or a permission slip.
