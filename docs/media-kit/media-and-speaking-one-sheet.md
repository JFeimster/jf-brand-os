## Full Bio
Jason Feimster is a U.S. Marine combat veteran, entrepreneur, and systems-minded operator focused on helping founders, freelancers, and underdog builders create more leverage through automation, smarter positioning, alternative funding strategy, and better business infrastructure.

He is the founder behind **Full-Time Hustler**, **Moonshine Capital**, and **Juliet Foxtrot Ventures**, where he develops content, prompts, workflows, brand systems, and entrepreneur-facing tools designed to help self-starters move from scattered hustle to practical leverage.

Jason’s perspective combines military discipline, founder realism, alternative-finance exposure, no-code experimentation, and a direct voice that values usefulness over polish-for-its-own-sake. He is especially well suited for conversations about AI for real operators, ownership-focused entrepreneurship, founder positioning, audience-owned media, systems thinking, and practical ways to build without waiting for institutional permission.
